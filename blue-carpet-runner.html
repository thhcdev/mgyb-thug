Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use blue carpet runners outdoors?
Yes, our blue carpets are suitable for outdoor use in good weather conditions.

How do I secure the carpet to prevent slipping?
We recommend using double-sided carpet tape for secure placement.

Are custom sizes available?
Absolutely! We offer custom sizing to fit your specific requirements.

How do I clean and store the carpet after use?
Clean any stains promptly, ensure the carpet is dry, roll it up with the fibers facing inward, and store in a dry place.
