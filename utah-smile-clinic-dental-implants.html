What Are Dental Implants?

Think of dental implants as artificial tooth roots. Made of titanium, they’re surgically placed into your jawbone to create a sturdy base for a crown, bridge, or even a full set of dentures. The best part? Over time, the implant fuses with your bone in osseointegration, making it incredibly strong and stable.

 

Key Benefits of Dental Implants

Are You a Good Candidate for Dental Implants?

The Dental Implant Process

Getting dental implants is a journey, but trust us—it’s worth it! Here’s what you can expect:

  1. Consultation and Planning: We’ll assess your oral health and create a personalized treatment plan.
  2. Placement Surgery: The titanium implant is inserted into your jawbone under local anesthesia.
  3. Healing Period: Over a few months, your jawbone will fuse with the implant.
  4. Abutment and Crown: A connector (abutment) is added, followed by your custom crown.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long do dental implants last?

Dental implants can last a lifetime with proper care, but the crowns may need replacement after 10-15 years.

2. Is the procedure painful?

Most patients say it’s less painful than they expected. Local anesthesia ensures you’re comfortable during the procedure.

3. What’s the cost of dental implants?

4. How do I care for my dental implants?

Treat them like your natural teeth! Brush twice a day, floss daily, and visit your dentist regularly.
