Cultivate House Detailing

Cultivate House Detailing was founded in 2022 by Joshua Mote. The ultimate goal and vision of Cultivate House Detailing is to provide convenience and excellent customer service and to cultivate the next generation of service industry professionals. Too often, we hear that service companies don’t get back to their customers in a timely manner or stand behind their work. Cultivate House Detailing is working to upgrade the customer service experience in Colorado Springs. There’s also a deeper need we are working to fill: the lack of trained service industry professionals. We are working to cultivate and train the next generation of service industry professionals for the long-term health of the industry and for the betterment of our beautiful city of Colorado Springs.
