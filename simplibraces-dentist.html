Top Tips for Healthy Teeth

Keeping your smile healthy doesn’t have to be a chore. Try these tips:

  • Brush twice a day with fluoride toothpaste.
  • Don’t forget to floss—it gets where your toothbrush can’t!
  • Limit sugary snacks and drinks.
  • Visit your dentist every six months for a check-up.
  • Wear a mouthguard during sports to protect your teeth.
