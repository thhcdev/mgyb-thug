What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are like superheroes for your teeth. They’re small titanium posts that act as artificial tooth roots, holding replacement teeth firmly in place. Unlike dentures that can slip or bridges that need other teeth for support, implants stand alone, giving you the next best thing to your natural teeth.

The Dental Implant Process

Getting dental implants may sound complicated, but it’s a straightforward process:

  1. Consultation and Evaluation: Your dentist will assess your oral health and discuss your options.

  2. Placement: The implant is placed into your jawbone, creating a sturdy foundation.

  3. Healing Time: The implant fuses with your jawbone over a few months.

  4. Crown Placement: A custom-made crown is attached to complete your new tooth.

Throughout the journey, Smile Clinic USA ensures you’re comfortable and informed every step of the way.
