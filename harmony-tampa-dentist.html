FAQs about Cosmetic Dentistry


What is the difference between veneers and dental bonding?

Veneers are thin porcelain shells placed over the teeth, while dental bonding involves applying a tooth-colored resin to reshape or repair the teeth.

 

How long does Invisalign treatment take?

In most cases, Invisalign treatments take between 12 and 18 months, depending on the complexity of the case.

Are dental implants permanent?

Yes, dental implants are a permanent solution for missing teeth, designed to last a lifetime with proper care.

 

How often should I get teeth whitening treatments?

Professional teeth whitening results can last up to a year or more, but touch-ups may be needed depending on your lifestyle habits (such as smoking or drinking coffee).

 

Can I combine different cosmetic procedures?

Yes! Many patients opt for a smile makeover that combines several treatments like veneers, teeth whitening, and Invisalign for a complete transformation.
