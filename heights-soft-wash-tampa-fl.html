FAQs


1. How often should I schedule pressure washing?
Most homes benefit from professional cleaning once a year. However, if you live in an area with heavy rain or humidity like Tampa, you might need it more often.

2. Is soft washing better than traditional pressure washing?
Yes! Soft washing uses low pressure and eco-friendly detergents to clean surfaces gently but effectively. It’s perfect for delicate areas like roofs.

3. Can I pressure wash my own house?
While it’s tempting, DIY pressure washing can cause damage if you’re not careful. Professionals know how to adjust the pressure and use the right techniques for each surface.
