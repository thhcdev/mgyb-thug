What Is a Red Carpet Runner, Anyway?

A red carpet runner is exactly what it sounds like — a long, plush strip of rich red fabric (or other colors, if you want a twist) that gets rolled out to welcome people with style. But it’s more than just something fancy to walk on.

 

Why Use One?

  • Sets the mood instantly
  • Defines entrances or walkways
  • Creates perfect photo opportunities
  • Elevates the entire event experience
  • Shows guests they’re valued

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should my red carpet runner be?
It depends on your event. For a wedding aisle, 25–50 feet is common. For a short entryway, 10–20 feet might be plenty. We offer custom sizes to match your needs.

Can I use these carpets outside?
Absolutely! Many of our runners are designed for outdoor use, with materials that resist wear and tear. Just make sure to anchor them properly.

Are red carpets just for celebrities?
Not at all! Red carpets are for anyone who wants to make an impression. Whether it’s your big day or a casual celebration, it adds a wow factor everyone loves.

Do you offer other colors besides red?
We sure do! While red is the classic, we have multiple color options for custom orders. Think gold, black, white, and more.
